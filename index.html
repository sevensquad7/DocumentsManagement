Technical Challenges

Challenge #0:
Database modeling: Initially, the lack of experience in database design and the company's workflow made modeling difficult.
Challenge #1:
Learning React: The learning curve of React is high, so we don't cover many concepts of that technology, we just use what is necessary to achieve the objectives.
Challenge #2:
Clean Code: Due to the various forms and working models that we find, which made it difficult for us to choose the one that adapts to our project.

Non-technical Challenges

Challenge #0:
Planning: We did not start in an orderly manner, at first we advanced skipping important steps, which produced several changes in our task programming, and in the structure of the database.
Challenge #1:
Communication, in the initial part of the project it was difficult for us to maintain order when communicating and being assertive when making our ideas known.
